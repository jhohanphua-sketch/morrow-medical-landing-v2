Your physiological reserve — not just whether you're sick

Standard health screenings look for disease. MORROW Insights measures physiological reserve — cardiovascular fitness, muscle mass, strength and power, lung function — validated markers linked to how well we age, regardless of whether you feel fine today.

What your reserve tells you to do next

Numbers without context are just noise. Your results come with a 90-minute educational group session where our clinical team walks through what each marker means and what it suggests about your health priorities going forward.

A single data point tells you where you are. A second point tells you which direction you're heading. MORROW Insights is designed as a meaningful starting point — something to come back to and measure your progress against.

Questions

Before you book.

What exactly am I buying?
A longevity-focused health screening — 40+ tests across cardiovascular fitness, body composition, strength and power, lung function, blood biomarkers, and psychological well-being. Done in a single visit (~2 hrs 40 mins). Followed by a 90-minute educational group session where our clinical team explains your results and what they mean for your health priorities. You receive a full results report. S$599. Screening conducted physically in Singapore.
Does the group session replace a doctor consultation?
No. The Educational Group Discussion is a structured educational session — not a medical consultation. It explains your results and the research behind each test. For a dedicated 1:1 review of your results with a doctor, that is available as a separate add-on after the group session.
Does this replace my GP?
No. MORROW Insights is designed to complement your existing healthcare. The detailed baseline we provide can be shared with your GP or specialist — most of what we measure isn't routinely tested. We flag anything that warrants further investigation.
